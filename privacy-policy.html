Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how the game Merge Miner ("the Game", "we", "us") handles information when you play it. The Game is distributed through the Poki platform.

1. Information We Collect

The Game itself does not require you to create an account and does not intentionally collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number. Basic gameplay progress may be stored locally on your device (for example, using your browser's local storage) so you can continue where you left off.

2. Information Collected by Poki

The Game is hosted and served by Poki. Poki may collect certain data (such as device type, browser information, IP address, and gameplay analytics) to operate the platform and serve advertisements. This data is governed by Poki's own privacy policy, available at https://poki.com/en/c/privacy-statement.

3. Advertising

The Game may display advertisements provided through the Poki SDK. These ads may use cookies or similar technologies. Ad personalization and data handling are managed by Poki and its advertising partners in accordance with their policies.

4. Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The Game is designed to be family-friendly. Any data handling related to younger players is managed through Poki's platform, which is built to comply with applicable regulations such as COPPA and GDPR-K.

5. Local Storage

The Game may save your progress and settings locally in your browser. This information stays on your device and is not transmitted to us. You can clear it at any time by clearing your browser data.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
